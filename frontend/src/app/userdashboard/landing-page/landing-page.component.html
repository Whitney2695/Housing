How It Works

Step 1: Register on the Platform

Sign up on Haven Home's platform to create your account and start saving for your future home. The registration process is simple, and once completed, you'll have access to exclusive housing projects and resources to guide you through your homeownership journey. Begin your savings with a small deposit to kickstart your path toward homeownership.

Register Now

Step 2: Choose Your Ideal Housing Project

Explore our variety of affordable housing projects located in prime areas. Browse through detailed information on each project, including location, pricing, and available units. Select the one that suits your preferences and financial situation, and start planning your future home with us. You’ll find comprehensive project descriptions and eligibility criteria for each listing.

Step 3: Calculate Your Mortgage Eligibility

Once you’ve chosen your ideal housing project, it's time to calculate your mortgage eligibility. Use our easy-to-use calculator to check your mortgage options based on your savings, income, and the selected housing project. We’ll help you determine the amount you qualify for, and you can then start making the necessary payments. Regular progress tracking will ensure you're on the right path, and you can test eligibility based on real-time backend data.
